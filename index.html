Alongside our usual coverage of politics and public affairs, the latest issue of agendaNi encompasses detailed reports on Brexit; policing and justice; agribusiness; Ards and North Down; and the social economy.

Highlights include a cover story discussion featuring Radius Housing around their future roadmap, PSNI deputy chief constable Stephen Martin discusses the future of policing and we visit Maghaberry Prison to talk with governor David Kennedy about progress in the prison. Additionally, DAERA permanent secretary Denis McMahon outlines the development of the Department around agribusiness and we have a special focus on Ards and North Down and the social economy.
